# Personas

## Agency Delivery Lead

Needs a white-label platform that can support multiple branded client launches without custom one-off builds. Cares about clean handoff, premium presentation, predictable operations, and self-hostability.

## Creator Team Operator

Needs fast turnaround from source asset to collectible campaign page. Cares about curation workflow, final asset quality, edition planning, and later onchain publication.

## Portrait or Event Studio Owner

Needs a branded productized upsell for client photos. Cares about guided asset preparation, quality control, and a premium storefront that feels more valuable than a basic gallery.

## NFT-Native Studio

Needs flexible blockchain infrastructure with room for bespoke 1/1s and edition drops. Cares about contract path, metadata integrity, collection presentation, and later reconciliation.
